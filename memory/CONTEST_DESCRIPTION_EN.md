# Ordia — Contest Description (English)

## One-liner
Ordia turns any order — from WhatsApp, email, PDF, Excel, photos or voice notes — into a clean, ERP-ready order. Automatically.

## Short pitch (2–3 sentences)
Wholesale distributors drown in orders that arrive in every possible format, and someone has to retype each one by hand — slow, error-prone and expensive. Ordia uses AI to read any incoming order from any channel, match products and quantities to the customer's catalog, and hand back a structured order in seconds. It doesn't replace your ERP — it works *with* it, removing the one job nobody wants: manual data entry.

## The problem
For food and goods wholesalers, orders come in as WhatsApp messages, emails, scanned PDFs, Excel sheets, photos of handwritten notes, even voice memos. A person reads each one and re-keys it into the management system. It's hours of repetitive work every day, it introduces typos and missed lines, and it pulls staff away from serving customers.

## The solution
Ordia is an AI-powered order automation platform:
- **Ingest anything:** paste text or upload PDF/Excel/CSV/image/audio — 9 input channels.
- **AI extraction:** it identifies the customer, products and quantities and matches them to your catalog (with learned aliases and formats).
- **Review only the doubts:** confident lines are done; you only confirm what the AI is unsure about. One click to approve.
- **ERP-ready output:** export to JSON, CSV, Excel or PDF, with configurable export profiles.
- **Ordia Bridge:** a lightweight on-prem agent that connects even to management systems *without an API*. It learns your procedure (Learning → Shadow → Ready → Active) and runs it for you.

## Why it's different
Ordia is **not another ERP**. It's the automation layer that sits in front of the ERP you already use. That positioning removes the biggest adoption barrier for SMBs: no migration, no rip-and-replace — just less typing, fewer errors, faster orders.

## Built with
React + FastAPI + MongoDB. Real AI extraction (Claude) and speech-to-text (Whisper). Fully bilingual (IT/EN). Multi-tenant, production-ready.

## Impact
From chaos to an order ready for your ERP — in seconds instead of minutes. That's Ordia.
